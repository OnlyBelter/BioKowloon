What is BioKowloon?
-------------------

BioKowloon is a versatile toolkit designed for the comprehensive analysis of multi-omics data, including single-cell RNA-seq, bulk RNA-seq, and the integration of multi-omics datasets.
Developed by Xin Xiong at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) in Kowloon Tong, Hong Kong,
this package is continuously evolving to meet the diverse needs of biologists and bioinformaticians in their daily research endeavors.
